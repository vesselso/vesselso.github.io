FAQ

->

Can I use Vessel with HKU Moodle?

Yes. Vessel is built on top of Moodle, so you can use Vessel like you would on Moodle.

Is Vessel free of charge?

Yes. Vessel is free of charge.

Is Vessel secure?

Short answer: yes. Your password and files are only stored on your computer, and are never transferred to our servers. Moreover, every password is encrypted with AES-256, the encryption standard used by banks and military.

How does Vessel get data from Moodle?

Vessel uses client-side web scraping, which allows the Vessel app on your computer to access the Moodle website securely and download course materials.
